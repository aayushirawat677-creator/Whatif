LIFE TRANSITIONS & MAJOR CHANGES - Research & Statistics

PSYCHOLOGY OF MAJOR LIFE CHANGES
- William Bridges' Transition Model: endings → neutral zone → new beginnings
- Average adjustment period for major life change: 18-24 months
- Identity disruption during transitions is normal and temporary
- People who frame transitions as "experiments" adapt 40% faster

EDUCATION DECISIONS
- Gap year students have higher GPA and graduation rates than non-gap year peers
- Adult learners (25+) complete degrees at higher rates than traditional students
- Online degree holders earn equivalent salaries to on-campus peers in most fields
- Bootcamp graduates report 74% employment in field within 6 months

HEALTH & LIFESTYLE CHANGES
- Exercise habit takes average 66 days to form (UCL study)
- People who move to new cities report 30% improvement in mental health within 1 year
- Dietary changes take 3-6 months to show measurable health outcomes
- Sleep optimization has highest ROI of any health intervention

GEOGRAPHIC MOVES
- 11% of Americans move each year
- International relocation increases life satisfaction scores by average 15%
- Expats report higher career advancement rates than domestic peers
- Moving for opportunity vs moving away from problems — success rate 70% vs 35%

PERSONAL GROWTH TIMELINES
- Meaningful skill acquisition takes 20 hours of deliberate practice (Josh Kaufman)
- Expert-level mastery requires 10,000 hours (Anders Ericsson)
- Most personal development books overestimate short-term change, underestimate long-term transformation
- Journaling for 15 minutes daily reduces decision regret by 31%

RESILIENCE RESEARCH
- Post-traumatic growth is more common than post-traumatic stress after major challenges
- Social support is the #1 predictor of successful navigation of life transitions
- People who share their goals publicly are 65% more likely to achieve them
- "Implementation intentions" (if-then planning) increase goal achievement by 91%
