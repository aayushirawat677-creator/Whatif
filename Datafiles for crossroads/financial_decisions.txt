FINANCIAL DECISIONS - Research & Statistics

INVESTING & WEALTH BUILDING
- S&P 500 average annual return: 10.7% over last 50 years
- Time in market beats timing the market — missing the 10 best days per decade cuts returns by 50%
- Starting to invest at 25 vs 35 results in 2x the retirement wealth at 65
- Index funds outperform actively managed funds 92% of the time over 15 years (SPIVA)
- Emergency fund recommendation: 3-6 months of expenses in liquid savings

REAL ESTATE
- Average US home appreciates 3-4% annually over long term
- Renting vs buying breakeven point: typically 5-7 years in the same location
- Transaction costs of buying/selling a home: 8-10% of home value
- Remote work has increased home values in secondary cities by 15-20%

EDUCATION INVESTMENT
- College degree premium: $1M additional lifetime earnings vs high school diploma
- Student loan debt averages $37,000 for bachelor's degree holders
- ROI varies dramatically by field: STEM degrees return 15% annually, arts degrees 7%
- Vocational training ROI can exceed traditional 4-year degree in 5 years

DEBT MANAGEMENT
- Average American carries $6,500 in credit card debt
- Avalanche method (high interest first) saves average $1,300 vs snowball method
- Mortgage refinancing breakeven: typically 2-3 years

FINANCIAL PSYCHOLOGY
- Loss aversion: people feel losses 2x more intensely than equivalent gains
- Sunk cost fallacy causes 73% of people to continue losing investments
- Financial stress is the #1 cause of relationship conflict
- People consistently overestimate their future income and underestimate expenses
