RELATIONSHIP & LIFE DECISIONS - Research & Statistics

MARRIAGE & PARTNERSHIP
- Couples who cohabit before marriage have a 33% higher divorce rate (CDC)
- Average age of first marriage: 30 for men, 28 for women (US Census 2023)
- Marriages where both partners have college degrees have 25% lower divorce rate
- Long-distance relationships succeed at the same rate as geographically close ones (70%)
- Couples who discuss finances before marriage are 2x less likely to fight about money

RELOCATION DECISIONS
- 27% of Americans move for relationship reasons
- People who move for a partner report 60% satisfaction rate after 2 years
- Moving to a new city increases social network size by average 40% within 3 years
- Remote work has increased willingness to relocate by 35% since 2020

FAMILY DECISIONS
- Average cost of raising a child to 18: $310,000 (USDA 2023)
- Parents report lower moment-to-moment happiness but higher sense of meaning
- Couples who wait until late 20s to have children report higher relationship satisfaction
- Blended families have 67% success rate when stepparent role is gradual

BREAKUPS & DIVORCE
- 50% of first marriages end in divorce; 67% of second marriages
- Average time from separation to divorce: 1 year
- Financial independence is the #1 predictor of successful post-divorce recovery
- 73% of people report being happier 2 years after a difficult breakup

PSYCHOLOGICAL RESEARCH ON RELATIONSHIPS
- "Gottman ratio" — 5 positive interactions needed to offset 1 negative
- Contempt is the #1 predictor of relationship failure
- Shared values matter more than shared interests for long-term compatibility
- People adapt to relationship changes (good and bad) faster than they expect
